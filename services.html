Our Comprehensive Services

We offer a full spectrum of clinical data management services, tailored to meet the unique needs of your research.

Data Collection & Validation

We design and implement robust eCRFs (Electronic Case Report Forms) and manage EDC (Electronic Data Capture) systems. Our processes include rigorous data validation checks to ensure accuracy and completeness from the outset.

  • CRF/eCRF Design and Development
  • EDC System Setup and Management
  • Data Entry and Query Management
  • Edit Check Programming and Validation

Clinical Database Design & Development

Our experts design and develop secure, compliant, and user-friendly clinical databases. We ensure that databases are optimized for data integrity, efficient querying, and regulatory requirements (e.g., CDISC standards).

  • Needs Assessment and Specification Development
  • Database Build and UAT (User Acceptance Testing)
  • Data Migration and Integration
  • CDISC SDTM/ADaM Implementation Support

Data Quality Control & Assurance

We implement comprehensive QC/QA plans to ensure data accuracy, consistency, and reliability. This includes medical coding (MedDRA, WHODrug), SAE reconciliation, and regular data reviews.

  • Development of Data Management Plan (DMP)
  • Medical Coding and SAE Reconciliation
  • Data Audits and Quality Checks
  • Database Lock Procedures

Regulatory Compliance & Reporting

Navigating the complex regulatory landscape is crucial. We ensure full compliance with international standards such as FDA 21 CFR Part 11, ICH-GCP, HIPAA, and GDPR. We also assist in preparing data for regulatory submissions.

  • SOP Development and Implementation
  • Compliance with 21 CFR Part 11, GCP, GDPR
  • Data Archiving and Retention
  • Support for Regulatory Submissions
